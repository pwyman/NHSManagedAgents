# Sample FOI Requests — NHS Westshire ICB

> **Purpose:** These 25 sample requests are used for testing the FOI Agent. Each is written as a realistic email a member of the public might send. They cover a range of scenarios including straightforward disclosures, s.21 referrals, partial disclosures, escalation triggers, EIR requests, and potentially vexatious requests.

---

2. All Board members' personal diaries for the last 6 months.
3. All text messages sent by Michael Chen mentioning any councillor or elected official.
